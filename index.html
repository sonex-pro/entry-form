Event details:

  • Approved by Table Tennis England and Essex County TTA
  • Date: Sunday, 2nd November 2025
  • Venue: BATTS TT Club, Norman Booth Centre, Elderfield, Mulberry Green, Old Harlow, Essex CM17 0EY
  • Referee: Neil Brierley (Club Manager)
  • Organiser: Carl Johnson (TTE Level 1 coach) – Tel: 07469 844024, Email: carl.johnson.batts@gmail.com

Prize fund:

  • Band 1 Winner £250, Runner Up £120, Semi-finalists £50 each
  • Band 2 Winner £50, Runner Up £40
  • Band 3 Winner £40, Runner Up £30

Entry fee: £35 (£30 BATTS fee + £5 umpire fee, refunded if you umpire at knockout)

Maximum 48 entries – accepted in order of receipt.

The tournament runs all day (approx. 9:15am–5:30pm). Players must be available for morning and afternoon sessions. Venue opens at 8:00am for registration & practice (play starts 9:15am).

Under 18s must be accompanied by an adult. The draw will be made on 5th November but not published in advance.
